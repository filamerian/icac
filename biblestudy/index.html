From Genesis to Exodus, we see a consistent pattern:

  • God initiates relationship.
  • Humanity rebels.
  • God responds with mercy and covenant.
  • Salvation is always by grace, through faith, and for the purpose of worship and holiness.

These books lay the groundwork for the gospel. They whisper the name of Jesus long before He is born. They show us that salvation is not a New Testament idea - it is the eternal plan of a loving God.
